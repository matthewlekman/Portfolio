Where do I want to be?

I aspire to become a Software Engineer for a company that aligns itself with similar values to ones I am predisposed with. To be apart of a team that contribute equally and each play to their strengths to produce the best output possible. I am particularly interested in working on meaningful projects which I have a genuine passion to fulfill. A flexible working lifestyle has always appealed to me. The access to culture and perspectives that remote work can offer is one of the things I desire to thrive from. I am a strong believer in getting out of your comfort zone and developing as you broaden your horizons.

What skills do I need?

After some time researching, it seems skills do not simply boil down to what technical skills you have. They branch into different types, in particular: soft skills. Soft skills, in my eyes, are the skills that help you work with others and become the best team member that you can be.
Those that I believe are essential are as follows:
  • Perseverance
  • Adaptability
  • Listening
  • Goal-oriented
  • Driven
  • Respect
  • Punctual
  • Communication

Having said that, I believe you can never stop developing your soft skills - and every soft skill is important.

As far as technical skills go, I think this is fairly straightforward.
  • Bachelors Degree
  • Competent in a variety of languages
  • Skilled with web design: HTML and CSS
  • Familiarity with practices such as version control (Git) and knowledge of methodology
  • Driven
  • Respect
  • Punctual
  • Communication
